Help & FAQs

How do I add an item?

Go to Manage Items, fill the form, and click Add Item.

How do I edit or delete items?

Use the Edit and Delete buttons in the item list.

How does search work?

Type a name in the search bar to find matching items.

Is my data saved permanently?

No, data is only stored temporarily in your browser session.

Who can I contact for support?

This is a student project. Contact your course instructor for assistance.
